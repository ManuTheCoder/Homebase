Todo list

The todo list is on the front page of KeepInventory. To get started, go ahead and click + New item After that, you can enter a new todo item by entering the due date, description, title, and priority. You can filter items by due date, priority, and you can remove checked items by clicking the Settings button, which is located right next to + New item

Installing the desktop app

You can download KeepInventory desktop version herehere
TO DOWNLOAD, and install

  1. Click the link above
  2. Go to chrome://extensions.
  3. Turn on Developer mode in the top right corner
  4. Drag and drop the application file (pramodini.github.io/kepinventory.crx) to the window.
  5. Enjoy! You can also pin it to your desktop if you want to!

Adding an item to your inventory

To add an item to your inventory, just type whatever you want in the text box. You can search if you want, also. ALTHOUGH ITEMS GET SAVED AFTER YOU LEAVE THE SESSION, IT GETS DELETED AFTER YOU CLEAR YOUR HISTORY. TO PREVENT THIS FROM HAPPENING,
Don't check the checkbox which says: "cookies and site data" when clearing your history

Deleting items from your inventory

To delete item(s) from your inventory, you can simply delete everything in the text box(es). You can delete your account by going to settings > Extreme measures> Delete account. You can also clear your history to delete items.
